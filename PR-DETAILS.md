# Flight Delay Insurance Platform Smart Contracts\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces a comprehensive decentralized flight delay insurance platform built on the Stacks blockchain. The platform provides instant, automated payouts based on real-time aviation data, eliminating traditional claim processes and ensuring transparent, blockchain-based insurance coverage.\n\n## Smart Contracts Implemented\n\n### 1. Flight Data Oracle (`flight-data-oracle.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Integration with FlightAware and airline APIs for delay verification\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Real-time flight status monitoring and data verification\n- Authorized oracle management for trusted data sources\n- Airline API registration and authentication\n- Flight delay calculation and validation\n- Comprehensive data integrity checks\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `update-flight-data`: Updates flight information with delay calculations\n- `verify-flight-delay`: Provides verified delay data for payout processing\n- `add-authorized-oracle`: Manages trusted data source permissions\n- `register-airline-api`: Registers airline APIs for data collection\n\n**Lines of Code**: 246 lines\n\n### 2. Instant Payout Processor (`instant-payout-processor.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Automated claim processing and payout for delays over threshold minutes\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Insurance policy creation and management\n- Automated payout processing based on delay thresholds\n- Manual claim submission and processing\n- Policy validation and expiry management\n- Contract fund management with secure transfers\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `create-policy`: Creates new insurance policies with coverage parameters\n- `process-automatic-payout`: Processes automated payouts for eligible delays\n- `submit-manual-claim`: Allows manual claim submission for edge cases\n- `deposit-funds`/`withdraw-funds`: Manages contract liquidity\n\n**Lines of Code**: 298 lines\n\n### 3. Premium Calculator (`premium-calculator.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Dynamic pricing based on route history and seasonal patterns\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Route-specific risk analysis and premium calculation\n- Airline performance tracking and premium modifiers\n- Seasonal risk factors and holiday adjustments\n- Historical premium tracking and analytics\n- Bulk data updates for efficiency\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `calculate-flight-premium`: Calculates dynamic premiums based on multiple risk factors\n- `update-route-statistics`: Updates route-specific delay statistics\n- `update-airline-performance`: Tracks airline reliability metrics\n- `set-seasonal-factors`: Configures seasonal risk adjustments\n\n**Lines of Code**: 385 lines\n\n## Architecture Highlights\n\n###
